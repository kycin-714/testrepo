## General info
_Lantern Rush_ is a single player platformer adventure game. After waking up in the middle of knowhere, you must navigate your way back home and pick up the decorations and items that are necessary for a proper Chinese New Year dinner! Jump through platform courses, dodge the spirits of the evil spirits and find the 6 hidden collectibles long the way.

## How to play
Use W A S D to control the player. Use [space] to jump. Walk over the collectibles to pick up.
